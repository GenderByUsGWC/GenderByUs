The Team


We hope to not only make those who play more self-aware, but also inspire them to fight these gender biases in their communities and societies.

As four teenage girls of different races and backgrounds, we have had very different life experiences, yet we all feel the effects of gender bias in our lives. After meeting at Girls Who Code, we wanted to build something that would help combat this very real problem in our society.
